Lubang is a group of islands situated in Occidental Mindoro and off the deep waters of the Verde Island Passage. An archipelago seemingly untouched by time. Lubang: An Island of Paradise, presents captivating photographs of a group of islands in Occidental Mindoro. Taking you on a journey into a serene place teeming with charm of nature and life.
